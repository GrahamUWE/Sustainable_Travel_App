## Welcome to UWE EcoCommute! 🌱

## 🚀 Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Open the App
- **On Mobile**: Open the app URL in your mobile browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)
- **On Desktop**: The app works on desktop too, but is optimized for mobile phones
- **No Account Needed**: Start using immediately - no sign-up required!

### Step 2: Log Your First Journey
1. Tap the **"Track"** tab at the bottom of the screen
2. Choose your transport mode (e.g., Walking, Cycling, Bus)
3. Enter the distance you traveled
4. See your instant impact preview
5. Tap **"Log Journey & Earn Rewards"**

### Step 3: Track Your Progress
- Check the **"Home"** tab to see your stats
- Visit **"Rankings"** to see where you stand
- Collect **"Badges"** as you hit milestones

## 🚗 Transport Modes Explained

### 🌟 Zero Emissions (Best!)
- **Walking 🚶** - 15 pts/km | 0 kg CO₂/km
- **Cycling 🚴** - 20 pts/km | 0 kg CO₂/km
  - *Highest points!*
  - *Perfect for short distances*

### ⚡ Low Emissions (Excellent)
- **E-Scooter 🛴** - 18 pts/km | 0.05 kg CO₂/km
- **Electric Car ⚡** - 10 pts/km | 0.053 kg CO₂/km
- **Train 🚆** - 13 pts/km | 0.041 kg CO₂/km
  - *Great for longer distances*

### 🟡 Moderate Emissions (Good)
- **Plug-in Hybrid 🔌** - 8 pts/km | 0.07 kg CO₂/km
- **Bus 🚌** - 12 pts/km | 0.089 kg CO₂/km
- **Motorbike 🏍️** - 5 pts/km | 0.113 kg CO₂/km
  - *Better than driving alone*

### 🔴 High Emissions (Try Alternatives)
- **Diesel Car 🚗** - 2 pts/km | 0.171 kg CO₂/km
- **Petrol Car 🚙** - 0 pts/km | 0.192 kg CO₂/km
  - *Track these to see what you could save by switching!*

## 💡 Common Questions

### "How do I measure distance?"
- Use Google Maps to check your route
- Estimate based on time (walking ≈ 5km/hour, cycling ≈ 15km/hour)
- For regular commutes, measure once and remember

### "What if I use multiple modes in one trip?"
- Log each segment separately
- Example: Walk to bus stop (0.5km walk) + Bus ride (3km bus)

### "Do partial journeys count?"
- Yes! Even 0.5km journeys earn points
- Every sustainable choice matters

### "Can I edit or delete journeys?"
- Currently not available - log carefully!
- Double-check mode and distance before submitting

### "Will my data sync across devices?"
- No, data is stored locally on your device
- Each device has its own profile
- Clearing browser data will reset progress

### "How often should I log journeys?"
- Log daily to maintain your streak
- Don't forget weekend journeys!
- Set reminders if needed

## 🌍 Environmental Impact

### Understanding Your Impact

**CO₂ Calculations**:
- All emissions compared to average petrol car (0.192 kg CO₂/km)
- Real-world emission factors used
- Savings = Baseline - Your mode

**Example Savings**:
```
5km by bike instead of car:
- CO₂ Saved: 0.96 kg
- Trees Equivalent: ~0.05 trees planted
- Points Earned: 100 points
```

**Annual Impact** (if you cycle 5km daily):
- Daily: 0.96 kg CO₂ saved
- Weekly: 6.7 kg CO₂ saved
- Monthly: 28.8 kg CO₂ saved
- Yearly: 350 kg CO₂ saved = 17.5 trees! 🌳

## 🚨 Troubleshooting

### App Not Loading?
- Check internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache
- Use updated browser version

### Lost My Data?
- Check if you're on the same device
- Data doesn't sync between devices
- Avoid clearing browser data

### Journey Not Logging?
- Ensure mode is selected
- Check distance is greater than 0
- Make sure form is complete

### Charts Not Showing?
- Log some journeys first
- Wait a few seconds for data to load
- Refresh the page
